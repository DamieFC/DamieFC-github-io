What I want to see in the C2X standard

Well, this is my first post, I hope it doesn't suck. Too bad.

At the end of this year, bigwigs and standard fanatics are gonna vote on the C2X standard. In case they see this, here are some things I want.

  • (#)print to be a preprocessor statement or keyword to print text
  • A built-in logging function or keyword or something
  • A string variable type

I have other things, and this may be updated. Still, it doesn't seem that hard.
